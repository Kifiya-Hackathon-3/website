About the wallet

Kifiya is a digital identity wallet built so you can prove who you are, store credentials safely, and share only what each service needs—without giving up control of your data.

This project is part of Hackathon 3.0, where we explore privacy-preserving identity and interoperability with modern standards such as the European Digital Identity framework.

Our focus is simple: your data, your control. The app is designed to make selective disclosure and secure storage the default, not an afterthought.

Source code and collaboration live on GitHub.
